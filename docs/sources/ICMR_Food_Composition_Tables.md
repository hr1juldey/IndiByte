# Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT) - ICMR-NIN

## Overview

The Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT) is a compilation of nutritional information for 528 key foods published by the Indian Council of Medical Research - National Institute of Nutrition (ICMR-NIN). This database serves as a critical resource for researchers, academics, dietitians, and public health professionals in India.

## Key Features

- Contains nutritional information for 528 key Indian foods
- Laboratory-verified nutrient information
- Widely used in nutrition research and public health
- Provides detailed nutritional profiles including calories, proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals

## Significance for IndiByte

The IFCT serves as a foundational resource for developing culturally appropriate and scientifically accurate dietary recommendations for the Indian population. This database addresses the need for India-specific nutritional data rather than relying on Western food composition tables that may not accurately reflect the nutritional content of traditional Indian ingredients.

## Publication Details

- Published by: Indian Council of Medical Research - National Institute of Nutrition (ICMR-NIN)
- First comprehensive compilation: 2017
- Contains data for: 528 key Indian foods
