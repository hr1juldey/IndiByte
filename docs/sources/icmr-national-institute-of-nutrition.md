# ICMR National Institute of Nutrition - Indian Food Composition Tables

## Overview

The Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT) is a comprehensive compilation of nutritional information for 528 key foods, widely used by researchers, academics, policy makers, and regulators across India and some South Asian countries. This database is maintained by the ICMR National Institute of Nutrition (NIN).

## Key Information

- **Number of foods**: 528 key foods documented
- **Primary use**: Research, academics, policy making, and regulation
- **Coverage**: India and South Asian countries
- **Published**: Most nutrients data published in 2017
- **Organization**: Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) - National Institute of Nutrition

## Significance for IndiByte

The IFCT serves as a foundational resource for nutritional data in India, providing standardized values that can be used to validate and enhance the IndiByte database. The data includes comprehensive nutritional profiles essential for building accurate food composition databases for Indian cuisine.

## Applications

- Research and academic studies
- Policy development
- Food regulation and safety
- Nutritional assessment and planning
- Dietary guideline development

## Availability

The IFCT is used by other South Asian countries as well, making it a valuable resource for regional nutritional standards.
