# Development of Indian Food Composition Database: Modern Approaches

## Overview
Recent research has focused on developing comprehensive Indian food composition databases using modern technological approaches. This includes work by researchers like Aswathy Vijayakumar and Hima Bindu Dubasi, who published research in June 2024 on developing an Indian Food Composition Database with advanced methodologies.

## Research Details
- **Authors**: Aswathy Vijayakumar and Hima Bindu Dubasi
- **Publication date**: June 2024
- **Title**: "Development of an Indian Food Composition Database" (part of research on retrieval augmented generation for smart calorie estimation)
- **Focus**: Creating comprehensive databases of Indian food nutritional content
- **Methodology**: Modern computational approaches for food composition analysis

## Technological Approaches
- **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)**: Using AI to improve calorie estimation in complex food scenarios
- **Smart calorie estimation**: Advanced algorithms for calculating nutritional content of mixed dishes
- **Database integration**: Combining traditional food composition data with modern technology
- **Image recognition**: Technology to identify and analyze food items
- **Natural language processing**: For processing food descriptions and recipes

## Database Components
- **Comprehensive food lists**: Extensive cataloging of Indian foods and preparations
- **Nutritional profiles**: Detailed macro and micronutrient information
- **Regional variations**: Documentation of state-wise and regional food differences
- **Preparation methods**: Impact of cooking methods on nutritional content
- **Mixed dishes**: Nutritional analysis of complex traditional preparations
