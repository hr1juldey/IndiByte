# Scene 11: The Scale of Impact - 10M+ Users by 2025

## Scene Description

This scene visualizes the ambitious goal of reaching 10M+ users by 2025, showing how IndiByte integrates with India's digital health infrastructure and serves as a foundational platform for nutrition services.

## Visual Elements

- Growth visualization from current users to 10 million targets
- Public health initiatives, clinics, and consumer apps using IndiByte
- Integration with Ayushman Bharat and other government health programs
- Foundation layer metaphor with other services built on top
- Timeline showing progress toward 2025 goal
- Network of services and platforms interconnected through IndiByte
- Growth chart showing user acquisition over time

## Character Animations

- Animated growth of users over time toward the 10 million target
- Different types of services and platforms connecting to the IndiByte foundation
- Public health workers and patients benefiting from the platform
- Government and healthcare systems integrating the platform
- Timeline animation moving toward 2025 milestone
